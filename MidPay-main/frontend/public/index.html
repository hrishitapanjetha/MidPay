What is MidPay?

     Significant number of online scams are observed every day and hundreds of innocent people are affected by it. We conducted a survey in which there were 78 people, out of which 50% of them were scammed online by fake social media sellers. The seller pretended to be a shop owner and accepted the payment online but the user never received the service he paid for. In order to prevent these scams, our team has come up with a solution by introducing a middleware between the seller and the customer, which accepts the payment from the customer, but doesn’t transfer it directly to the seller unless the services offered by him/her are fulfilled.

     We present MidPay - a Modern Solution to Fair Trade as an online portal built using NodeJS, ReactJS, MongoDB and an algorithm to calculate the genuinity of a seller using machine learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP) which will handle end-to-end payments to solve this problem. The user and seller can interact with each other and the users can purchase the products and services from a third party seller without worrying about their payment as it will be safe with us till the user receives the goods and services from the seller. Identification of a seller’s genuinity is a complicated task and we aim to accomplish this by a KYC (Know your Customer) system through which the seller can sign up on our portal using his valid government issued identity cards such as Aadhar. Unlike e-commerce giants like Amazon, Flipkart etc. people don't usually trust small businesses with money payment before delivery. If a middle payment gateway exists which makes sure that neither people nor the retailer have to worry about false credit practices, genuine small businesses will actually be promoted.
